Follow-Up Update – Website Access & Email Delivery
This is a brief follow-up regarding DM website access and email delivery. We are actively monitoring the situation, and everything is functioning smoothly. At this time, we are not seeing any new emails being routed to spam. Please note that some older email threads that were previously flagged may still go to Spam/Junk. Please review your spam folders carefully, and ensure you go in and mark the email "Not Spam" to help deliver future messages to your inbox. Please notify IT at helpdesk@dmclinical.com if you notice any irregularities. Thank you. IT & Cyber Security Department DM Clinical Research
